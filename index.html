About the App

Life On Track was built with the idea that you can track all of your habits, scheduling, and daily tasks in one place. The app makes it easy to create, manage, and monitor habits all from a single page. It's designed to help you get your life on track.

About the CEO

Hi! I'm Isaira Alvarez, a student at Boston University. This is my Habit Tracker App project for CM523, created as a way to apply everything learned this semester, from HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to building a friendly and sustainable app.
